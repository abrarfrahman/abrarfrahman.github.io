MUSIC

Since elementary school, I have been passionate about music and playing musical instruments. To that end, I have been a part of the CHS Proud Pioneer Marching Band since my freshman year, playing the Alto Saxophone, an instrument which I have now been playing for the last six years. In addition, since my sophomore year, I have played with the highest ranking music class available, the Wind Ensemble. Additionally, I have publicly performed on the Soprano Saxophone, Tenor Saxophone, Baritone Saxophone, and the guitar. In the 7th and 8th grades, I lead a Saxophone Trio, which earned medals of commendation at the CMEA Solo-Ensemble Festival. Currently, I am leading a group of my fellow musicians to perform Saxophone Choir music in public, and we have our first performance scheduled for March 6, 2018. I have been willing to invest a great deal of my time into pursuing music, because I feel that it is important for everyone, regardless of their career choices, to have interests outside of work. Music has always been one of mine.
